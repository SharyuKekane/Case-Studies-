About Me

Designing for humans,

I'm a third-year Computer Science and Design student deeply interested in Product Design, Product Management, and technology-driven solutions that genuinely improve people's lives.

My approach bridges analytical thinking with creative empathy — I believe the best products emerge when you truly understand your users' context, constraints, and goals before writing a single line of code or sketching a wireframe.

When I'm not designing or building, you'll find me at hackathons, contributing to open-source at FOSS Club, or exploring how design can make complex technology feel effortlessly human.
